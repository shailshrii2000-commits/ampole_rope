About This Product

HDPE Five Colour Rope by AMPOLE is manufactured from premium high-density polyethylene, offering excellent durability, chemical resistance, and strength. These ropes are lightweight, weatherproof, and float on water, making them ideal for a wide range of applications.

  • Material: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)
  • Type: 3-strand twisted rope
  • Diameter: 2 mm to 24 mm
  • Length: 100 m, 200 m, 300 m (customizable)
  • Color: Multiple colours / custom colours available
  • Filament: Monofilament

Physical & Chemical Properties

  • High strength and durability
  • Minimal water absorption & floats on water
  • Weatherproof & chemical resistant
  • Controlled elongation under load
  • Lightweight & easy to handle

Common Applications

  • Industrial lifting & rigging
  • Marine & fishing applications
  • Construction material securing
  • Transportation load tying
  • Domestic & general-purpose use
